OUR STORY

A LEGACY IN THE MAKING

Our History

Rooted in strong family values, LOGS Wine is more than a business; it is a legacy in the making, built with dedication, passion, and a commitment to excellence.

Born from the shared vision of four siblings—Lavida, Olivia, Godswill, and Splendor—LOGS Wine carries the very essence of their initials and unbreakable family bond, a heartfelt tribute to unity, ambition, and African pride. Founded in South Africa’s vibrant Western Cape and nurtured through Logs Investment since 2016, this brand transforms the continent’s rich terroir into accessible, soulful wines that bridge heritage and modern enjoyment.

Their father, John Eriourolo, a visionary entrepreneur, ignited his passion for business through his deep appreciation for farming—transforming a seed into a bountiful harvest. With this inspiration, he dedicated himself to producing high-quality wines sourced from sustainable vineyards in South Cape, Western Cape.

OUR VALUES

  • Sustainability: Committed to eco-friendly practices.
  • Quality: Only the finest ingredients and methods.
  • Passion: Driven by love for winemaking.
  • Innovation: Blending tradition with modern techniques.

We believe in creating wines that not only taste exceptional but also tell a story of dedication and craftsmanship.

OUR VISION

At LOGS Wine, our mission is to offer affordable luxury to wine lovers while introducing new enthusiasts to the distinctive taste of our wines. We strive to establish LOGS Wine as a household name—a brand that captivates hearts, minds, and palates. Beyond crafting exceptional wines, we are committed to fostering sustainable employment opportunities and nurturing international partnerships that extend beyond Africa.
