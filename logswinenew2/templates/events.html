At LOGS Wine, we're passionate about more than just crafting exceptional wines — we're building moments, connections, and memories.

We're excited to bring our Sweet Red, Rosé Nectar, and Merlot to life through exclusive tastings, intimate dinners, pop-up experiences, and vibrant events across Nigeria — especially in Lagos and beyond. From beachside wine & food festivals to sophisticated tastings and live music nights paired with our signature blends, LOGS events are designed to celebrate elegance, flavor, and community.

Our first official events were during the festive seasons of late 2025 and we will continue in 2026. Stay tuned — we'll be announcing ticket details, dates, venues, and special guest collaborations right here very soon.
